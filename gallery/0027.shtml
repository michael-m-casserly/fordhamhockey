Mike Hearon, Class of 2007


October 2009 -- Mike Hearon, Class of 2007, finished up flight school and has been designated as a Naval Helicopter Pilot.
Mike has received his wings and was selected to fly the MH-60S Knighthawk.
He will be headed to San Diego, CA for 7 months to learn how to fly that helicopter, and then will move to Guam for a 3 year tour.
