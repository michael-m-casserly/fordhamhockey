2006 Fordham Hockey Charity Auction

You're going to donate money to the Fordham Hockey Club anyway, right? Now you can give 'em some cash AND get something valuable in return!

This year's auction features some stunning sweaters donated by the NHL (2), ECHL, Central Hockey League and an alum's private collection.

Two sweaters are "game-ready" World Cup sweaters. These sweaters are authentic game day sweaters made for use in the 2004 World Cup of Hockey. The jerseys are authenticated as game-ready with a special bar code located on the inside hem of the sweater.

These sweaters are one-of-a-kind and extremely rare. They were made available to NHL staffers through a private charity sale in December and would fetch at least $250.00 in public auction.

There's also an NHL game-weight Philadelphia Flyers jersey signed by forward Simon Gagne.

The ECHL and CHL each donated game-weight All-Star sweaters; both have unique designs.

All auction items will be available for review and bidding through the alumni game at the rink on March 25th. Please stop by and place a bid for these great items...the pictures here are just a taste...they're even better in person.
