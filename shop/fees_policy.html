Our Return Policy

All sales through the Fordham Hockey Shop are final. If you order the wrong color item, you may exchange it for the correct color within 30 days of the purchase. In these cases, you (the buyer) will be responsible for any applicable Shipping & Handling Fees.

If you receive an incorrect item (i.e. you order "Maroon", but receive "White"), you will be able to return the incorrect item to the Fordham Hockey Shop within 30 days. The correct item will then be shipped to you. You (the buyer) will not be responsible for any Shipping & Handling Fees.

Any questions, comments, or concerns regarding Shipping & Handling or the Fordham Hockey Shop Return Policy should be sent to fordhamramshockey@gmail.com. Thank you for visiting the Fordham Hockey Shop, we appreciate your support.
