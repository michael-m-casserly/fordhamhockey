Fordham Hockey Calendar Available For Sharing via Google Calendar
By Michael Casserly / Fordham Hockey Online

Aug 28, 2013 -- The Fordham Hockey Calendar is now available for sharing via Google Calendar. Instead of copying and pasting the events into your own calendar application, you can actually link directly to the calendar via the following options below.

October 5, 2015 UPDATE -- Google has discontinued XML support for Google Calendar. You may still access Google Calendar via any of the 3 options listed below.

1) You can copy and paste this into any calendar product that supports the iCal format:
https://www.google.com/calendar/ical/l0e69iac2j9snn7m073jd9er0o%40group.calendar.google.com/public/basic.ics

2) Google Calendar users can embed the Fordham Hockey Calendar into their own calendar by selecting "Other calendars", then "Add by URL", and then follow the prompts on the screen. You can refer to the iCal URL link above when prompted.

3) For any other applications, if you get prompted for Calendar ID, here it is:
Calendar ID: l0e69iac2j9snn7m073jd9er0o@group.calendar.google.com
