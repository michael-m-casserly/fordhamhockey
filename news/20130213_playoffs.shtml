MCHC Playoffs in Newark this Holiday Weekend
By Drew Mola / Fordham Hockey Online

Feb 16, 2013 -- For the 13th consecutive year, the Rams have qualified for the post-season.

1 - Admission is $ 5 for all games

Please remember that any money generated from the playoffs will go towards defraying costs for every team next season.

Parking should be $ 4 but you must park in the VIP section which is on the same block as the Pru Center (Broad and Lafayette). Please mention you are there to attend the hockey game in case there are any issues.

2 - Game format

Semi-final games
10 minute warm-up on clean ice and right into the start of the game
Ice cut between every period
If necessary...10 minute OT (4 on 4) - then a 3-man shootout

Finals
10 minute warm-up on clean ice and right into the start of the game
Ice cut between every period
20 minute OT (5 on 5)...Game time decision on what happens (if still tied) will be based on available time remaining in slot.
