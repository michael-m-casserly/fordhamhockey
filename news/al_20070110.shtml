Hockey Players Needed for Connecticut League
By Michael Casserly / Fordham Hockey Online

Jan 10, 2007 -- If there are any Fordham Hockey Alum who are interested in playing in a Connecticut Men's League, please contact Rob Loh (Class of 2003) as soon as possible. The league starts playing in early February as has a 16-game season followed by playoffs. Games will be played in the evenings on weeknights. Rob's team is currently looking to add a player or two and is in need of a goalie.

For more details, you can visit the following web site http://www.sportscenterct.com.

All interested players should please contact Rob at rloh@mahoneycohen.com.
