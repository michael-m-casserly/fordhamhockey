Fordham Advances to MCHC Championship
By Drew Mola / Fordham Hockey Online

Feb 26, 2006 -- Fordham defeated Hofstra by a 8-5 final in last night's semi-final.

For the first time in team history they will advance to the championship game!

They will face Albany State in the finals Sunday, Feb 26 at the Rinx in Hauppauge Long Island.

Albany defeated Fairfield by a 8-0 score.

Game time 3:15pm

Fordham and Albany State have only met once and the Rams lost 5-1 earlier this semester.
