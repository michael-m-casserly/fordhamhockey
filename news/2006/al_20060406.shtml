Wedding Bells for Eric Genau, FC '98
By Michael Casserly / Fordham Hockey Online

Apr 6, 2006 -- Congratulations are in order for Eric Genau FC '98. Eric and fiancee Sarah Joyce were recently engaged in Toronto, Canada. Sara is a Trinity College graduate, and like Eric, a native of Buffalo, NY. The couple has yet to set a date, but the wedding will be in Buffalo, where they will reside. Eric is also happy to announce that Sarah is a hockey fan and is looking forward to attending many future Fordham alumni games.
