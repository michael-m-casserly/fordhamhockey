Fordham Defeats Columbia to Capture the 2009-2010 MCHC Championship
By Drew Mola / Fordham Hockey Online

Feb 21, 2010 -- MCHC Championship Game Results

FORDHAM 6

COLUMBIA 5

What a great game and great effort from both teams!

Fordham jumped out to an early 1-0 lead but trailed for most the way.

Down 3-1 after 1, down 4-2 after 2, the Rams rallied back with goals at 10:45 and 6:46 mark to tie the game at 4.

Columbia answered back with a goal at 5:11 to once again go ahead, but the Rams answered back again 45 seconds later to tie the game at 5.

The Rams went ahead to stay with 3:33 left in the game and hung on to capture their second MCHC championship.
