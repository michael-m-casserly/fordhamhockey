Fordham Alumni is in Need of Our Help
By Drew Mola / Fordham Hockey Online

October 3, 2017

Dear Fellow Alumni,

Former Ram John Quigg (Class of 2013) was in a recent diving accident were he suffered paralysis.

The former Chaminade HS graduate and East Williston Long Island native was one of the most colorful players I have ever run across during my 34 years with the team.

I am asking the Fordham Hockey community to reach out and do whatever they can to support John and his family.
