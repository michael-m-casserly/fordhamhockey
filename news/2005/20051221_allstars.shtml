Fordham Places 4 All-Stars
By Drew Mola / Fordham Hockey Online

Dec 21, 2005 -- Four Fordham University players were named to this year's MCHC All Star team. The game will be held on January 14 at the Pepsi Arena in Albany NY at 2:00 PM prior to an Albany River Rats (New Jersey Devils American Hockey League Affiliate) game. Game admission is $10 per person and will include admission to the River Rats game. The team rosters format will be the MCHC Public Schools vs. the MCHC Private Schools.

The Fordham players named were Junior goaltender Joe Wichowski, Sophomore defensemen Mike Connolly, and Junior forwards Matt Pangia and John Stachulski.
