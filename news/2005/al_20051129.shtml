Calling All Goalies...
By Drew Mola / Fordham Hockey Online

Nov 29, 2005 -- Joe Donovan, FC '90, Fordham's leading scorer, is coaching a Mite Minor team in Brewster, NY and needs a part-time goalie coach for his team. Also, the Brewster Rink would like to have a goalie coach for all age groups to conduct a weekly goalie clinic.

Any interested parties can contact Joe by email at Joe_Donovan@Praxair.com or by phone at 203-837-2437.
