MCHC Honors Fordham Player
By Drew Mola / Fordham Hockey Online

Nov 2, 2005 -- The M.C.H.C. named Fordham goalie Joe Wichowski 'FC 07 the player of the month for October 2005.

Joe had a record of 5-1 during the month with a goals against average of 1.43, a save percentage of 95.1 %, and 2 shutouts.

Only a junior, he has already won more games than any other goalie in Fordham history (32-13-4) with a lifetime 2612 minutes played (2nd all time), a 90.6% save percentage, and 52 lifetime appearances (2nd all time).
