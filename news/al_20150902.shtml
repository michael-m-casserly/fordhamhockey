Looking for Defensemen to Play in MHL / Long Island, Fall 2015 Season
By Michael Casserly / Fordham Hockey Online

September 2, 2015

Dear Fellow Alumni,

If anyone is looking to skate this Fall, my team is looking for defensemen who are interested in playing in the Midnight Hockey League in Long Island. It's a 12 game season that starts around Sept. 23rd, with 1 game a week, mostly all Wednesdays. Games are played in different locations throughout Long Island. Our team will play primarily out of Freeport, Cantiague, Syosset, and Bethpage, with an occasional game at Parkwood (Great Neck) or Iceland (New Hyde Park). Games would typically start some time between 10 to 11PM.

Anyone who is interested in playing, please email Mike Casserly at fordhamramshockey@gmail.com We're currently looking for 2-3 defensemen to fill out our roster.

Thanks,
Mike Casserly, Class of 1999
