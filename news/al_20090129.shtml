Rich Libero Joins Comcast SportsNet
By Michael Casserly / Fordham Hockey Online

January 29, 2009 -- Rich Libero, FC '87, has joined Comcast SportsNet Philadelphia as Vice President of Digital Media. Libero joins CSN Philly after nearly 10 years at the National Hockey League where he built NHL.com into the top hockey website in the world.
