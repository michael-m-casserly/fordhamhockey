MCHC All-Star Game (Jan. 14)
By Drew Mola / Fordham Hockey Online

Jan 8, 2007 -- The Fordham University hockey team placed 4 players on this year's M.C.H.C. All-Star roster.

The game, which will be played on Sunday January 14 @ 2:00 pm at the Long Island Skating Academy (also known as Ice Works) in Syosset, Long Island, features teams from the MCHC North Conference facing off against team from the South Conference.

Two of the Rams -- Senior Left Wing Steve Barrett (8-7-15) and Junior Defensemen Mike Connolly (2-1-3) -- make their first ever appearance in all-star competition while Junior Center Matt Pangia (13-6-19) and Senior Goalie Joe Wichowski (7-3-0 3.47 goals against average, 89.5 save percentage) both make their third appearance in this league sponsored All-Star game.
