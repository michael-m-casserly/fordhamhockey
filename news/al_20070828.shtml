Former Team Captain to Wed
By Drew Mola / Fordham Hockey Online

August 28, 2007 -- Congratulations are in order for former team captain and all time Fordham penalty leader Andy Montroy FC' 00. Andy and fiancée Tami were recently engaged in Maine and have set a September 2008 wedding date. Andy and his future wife currently reside in Philadelphia where Andy is currently employed by the Defense Association of Philadelphia.
